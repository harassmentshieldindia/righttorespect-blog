About the Initiative

Learn about our mission to support victims of loan recovery harassment

I am not a legal professional - I am a victim of harassment just like you. Having experienced the trauma of aggressive loan recovery tactics firsthand, I understand the fear, stress, and helplessness you feel. This platform was born from my own struggle and determination to help others facing similar harassment.

Our Mission:

  • Provide free email support to harassment victims
  • Build a community of survivors supporting each other
  • Connect victims with qualified legal professionals
  • Raise awareness about borrowers' rights and dignity

Important Note: We don't charge any fees. This initiative runs entirely on voluntary donations from supporters who believe in our cause. Every rupee goes toward reaching more victims and bringing qualified legal professionals into our network.
